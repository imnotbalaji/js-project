Calm Money: A simple stock visualizer


Motivation: UX of almost all stock apps is meant for a pro user 100s of indicators, multiple charts! We start with the philosophy that a beginner wants simplicity and focus - a simple way to navigate to a stock and study its price movements. Other features are a footnote to this.


Navigation: It's simple.

  1. Search for your ticker. Choose a best match and Go!
  2. Zoom In / Zoom Out to see performance over different time periods
  3. Your "daily price close" chart is here - study and understand!
    • Color indicates price movement during that time period
    • Hover to get the price at different dates
  4. Breathe!


Note: This is a 1 week alpha version and I will keep adding features. Email me if you have any feedback or feature requests!
